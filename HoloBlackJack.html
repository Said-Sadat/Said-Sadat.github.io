HoloLens Gen 1
Black Jack

Created in Unity with C# for the HoloLens

This project was created for my final year dissertation. The research was on the impact AR had on Immersive experiences. It was created on the HoloLens gen 1 using Unity.
The game created was a simple Black Jack game where the player would play and try to achieve a win streak.
